Admissions

Start your child’s learning journey with Hadiqatul Eman Private School.

Hadiqatul Eman Private School welcomes students who are ready to learn, grow, and build a strong future through quality education, character development, English learning, STEAM activities, and Islamic values.

We aim to support every student based on their needs and talents. High-achieving students may receive school support such as books, notebooks, and learning materials to encourage their continued success.

Entry Requirements

  • Completed registration form
  • Student identification information
  • Previous school records, if available
  • Parent or guardian contact information
  • Recent student photo

Student Support

Students who need extra help in Mathematics, Science, English, or Islamic Studies may receive additional academic support. More curriculum details will be added later.

Sibling Discount

Families with more than one child enrolled may be eligible for sibling discounts. Please contact the school office for details.

School Facilities

Students benefit from a safe learning environment, organized classrooms, school activities, transportation support, and teacher guidance.
