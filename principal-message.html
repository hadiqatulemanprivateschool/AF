Principal’s Message

Message from the Principal

Welcome to Hadiqatul Eman Private School. It is my great honor to lead a school that is dedicated to shaping the future of our students through education, character development, and innovation.

Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment where students can grow academically, socially, and personally. We believe that every child has the potential to succeed, and it is our responsibility to guide them toward excellence.

At our school, we focus on modern education including STEAM learning, English language development, and interactive teaching methods. We aim to prepare our students not only for exams, but for real-life challenges.

We strongly believe in collaboration between teachers, parents, and students. Together, we can build a strong foundation for lifelong learning and success.

Thank you for trusting Hadiqatul Eman Private School.

Dr. Khalid Nikmal Wardak

Principal of Hadiqatul Eman Private School
